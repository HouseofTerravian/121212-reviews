Accessibility Statement

Last updated: March 2025

Our Commitment

MCM Enterprises is committed to ensuring that 121212.reviews is accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, and we continuously work to improve the accessibility of our Platform.

Measures We Take

To support accessibility, we have incorporated the following practices into our design and development:

  • Semantic HTML: We use proper heading hierarchy (h1–h4), landmark elements (nav, main, footer, section), and meaningful alt text for images where present.
  • Keyboard navigation: All interactive elements — navigation links, buttons, form fields, and filter controls — are reachable and operable via keyboard.
  • Color contrast: Text and interactive elements are designed to meet or exceed a 4.5:1 contrast ratio against their backgrounds.
  • Focus indicators: Visible focus states are maintained on all interactive elements for keyboard and screen reader users.
  • ARIA labels: Navigation buttons (including the mobile hamburger menu) and icon-only controls include descriptive aria-label attributes.
  • Responsive design: The Platform is fully responsive and usable on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.
  • No auto-play media: No audio or video content plays automatically without user initiation.
  • Form accessibility: All form fields are associated with descriptive labels. Required fields are indicated. Error messages are descriptive and associated with the relevant field.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some areas of the Platform may not yet meet all WCAG 2.1 Level AA criteria. Known areas we are actively working to improve include:

  • Star rating interactive inputs — we are evaluating improved keyboard and screen reader patterns for the star rating widget on the reviewer application form
  • Third-party linked content — product pages, merchant sites, and ecosystem platforms linked from reviews are operated independently and may have their own accessibility limitations outside our control

Supported Technologies

121212.reviews is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, TalkBack)
  • Browser zoom up to 200% without loss of content or functionality
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • High-contrast browser/OS modes

Feedback and Contact

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of 121212.reviews. If you encounter barriers, experience difficulty accessing any content, or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: accessibility@121212.reviews
  • General support: support@121212.reviews

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days and will work with you to provide the information or functionality you need through an accessible alternative if possible.

Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility concern, you may contact the relevant national or regional accessibility authority in your jurisdiction. In the United States, you may file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Justice Civil Rights Division or the U.S. Access Board.

Continuous Improvement

We review our Platform's accessibility regularly and update this statement to reflect any changes. Our goal is to ensure that every person, regardless of ability, can fully participate in the 121212 community and benefit from the reviews, information, and opportunities available on this Platform.
