About the Platform

About 121212.reviews

A trusted review network built on real purchases, real people, and real accountability.

121212.reviews exists because the Black community deserves a review platform that actually knows what it's reviewing.

When you search for a natural hair product, an Afrobeats album, or a Black-owned restaurant in Atlanta, the reviews you find were almost certainly written by people outside the culture — or by no one at all. The gap between what the community needs to know and what existing platforms tell them is enormous.

121212.reviews closes that gap. Every reviewer is a verified buyer within the 121212 ecosystem. Every review is written from lived experience — not a press kit, not an algorithm, not a paid placement.

And because we believe that cultural knowledge should be economically rewarded, every reviewer earns affiliate income on the purchases they drive. Your opinion has value. We make sure you get paid for it.

The Affiliate Model

Every review on 121212.reviews contains the reviewer's unique affiliate link. When a reader clicks that link and makes a purchase within 30 days, the reviewer earns a commission — automatically, without any additional action required. Payouts are processed monthly. There is no cap on earnings.

We require full disclosure on every review page and throughout the site. Our community knows when a link is an affiliate link — and they trust it anyway, because the review is real.

The Federated Review Network

121212.reviews is being built as a federated network. In Phase 2, curators will be able to own city- and category-specific subdomains — hair.atlanta.121212.reviews, music.chicago.121212.reviews, food.nola.121212.reviews. Each hub will be run by a locally embedded curator who knows their city's scene, their community's needs, and which products actually deliver.

This is the same model powering 121212.community — the federated city hub network — applied specifically to reviews. The .community hub for a city is the front door. The .reviews hubs go deeper, category by category.

Part of the 121212 Ecosystem

121212.reviews is one of six platforms in the 121212 ecosystem — alongside 121212.market, 121212.music, 121212.fun, 121212.law, and 121212.culture. Reviews surface directly on product and event pages across all six sites. Every purchase made through a review link benefits both the reviewer and the ecosystem as a whole.

The platform is operated by MCM Enterprises. For questions, partnership inquiries, or reviewer applications, contact us at reviews@121212.reviews.
